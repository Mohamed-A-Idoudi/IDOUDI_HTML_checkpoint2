Tunisian Delights Menu

Appetizers

  • Brik

    Traditional Tunisian pastry filled with tuna, egg, parsley, and capers, deep-fried until crispy.

  • Merguez

    Spicy lamb sausage flavored with harissa, served with grilled vegetables and couscous.

  • Tunisian Salad

    A refreshing mix of tomatoes, cucumbers, onions, olives, and feta cheese, dressed with olive oil and lemon juice.

Main Courses

  • Couscous Royale

    Steamed couscous served with a variety of meats such as lamb, chicken, and merguez sausage, accompanied by vegetables and chickpeas.

  • Makrouna

    A savory pasta filled with tomato, meat, onions, and capers.

  • Mloukhia

    A traditional Tunisian stew made with jute leaves cooked with lamb or chicken, served with rice or bread.

Desserts

  • Baklava

    Layers of phyllo pastry filled with chopped nuts and sweetened with honey syrup.

  • Makroudh

    Deep-fried semolina dough filled with dates, flavored with orange blossom water and dusted with powdered sugar.

  • Yoyos

    Traditional Tunisian cookies made with almond paste and flavored with lemon zest, shaped into rings and dusted with icing sugar.
